Who we are?

Welcome to Srinithi Enterprises Pvt Ltd, a trailblazer in the dairy industry with roots dating back to 1978. From our inception as Dhanalakshmi Traders to the establishment of Srinithi Enterprises Pvt Ltd in 2009, we've been committed to delivering high-quality dairy products. Under the visionary leadership of Mr. C. Dhandapani and Anguraj, our third-generation entrepreneurs, we have achieved a remarkable turnover of Rs. 595.43 Crores in FY 23-24. Recognized as the Best Buyer by NCDFI, our success is built on a foundation of excellence and industry recognition. Explore our diverse dairy offerings, including milk, condensed milk, butter, and more. With a robust business model and a vision to become a prominent leader in the national dairy market, we invite you to join us in experiencing the pinnacle of quality and reliability in the dairy industry, where tradition meets innovation for a sustainable future. Our commitment to transparency and strong relationships, as well as our active engagement in e-auctions and plans for expansion into Agri and Agro Products, positions us for continued success. We ensure swift delivery across Pan India, actively contribute to nationwide accessibility through import and export, and are set to establish new manufacturing plants across various states in India. With a turnover goal of Rs. 1000 Crores, our dedication to improvement and customer-centric strategies defines us as a trusted and influential presence in the dynamic dairy market.
